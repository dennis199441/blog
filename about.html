About Me

This is WHAT I do and WHY I do.

Hello World! This is Dennis from Hong Kong. Welcome to my personal blog that records new stuff I learnt recently.

I am currently an Analyst Programmer at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University working on web application development. I really enjoy working on software development as it always brings me a sense of accomplishment! Not only because of building my own application, but also because of solving users' problems. That's why I love software development. It allows me to use my knowledge to help other people.

Regarding my academic background, I was not a computer science guy (nor a good student LOL). I majored in System Engineering and Engineering Management and minored Economics at The Chinese University of Hong Kong. I did't like coding at that time and that's why I did't choose CS as my major. Throughout my U-life, over 80% of my coursework was mathematics and statistics (included econ and finance courses that I took). I have never thought of being a programmer in the future.

The turning point of my life is the summer in Year 3. I interned at Census and Statistics Department in Hong Kong. During my internship, I was required build some office internal tools to automate their admin process using VBA. In order to get this job done, I have to learn VBA by myself and start building those tools. It turns out that those tools work pretty good and the users are quite satisify with them.

After completing my intern, I found that I really enjoy working on programming and software development and so I started working as a programmer after I graduate.

As I am working in technology field, I have to keep learning new things and update myself constantly. That's why I setup this blog to record what I learnt.

Enjoy~~
